# Character Development: Personas as Story Anchors

## Summary
Define personas and character types to anchor stories and guide user empathy and decision-making.

## Persona Types
- **The Pilgrim** (seeking spiritual experience)
- **The Scholar** (seeking historical detail)
- **The Traveler-Family** (needs practical logistics)
- **The Visual Storyteller** (interested in photography/imagery)
