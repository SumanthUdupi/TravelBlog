Bhubaneswar â€¢ Temple City

Our first spiritual anchor was the Lingaraj Temple, the crown jewel of Kalinga architecture. Standing 180 feet tall, the Deula dominates the skyline. Unlike other Shiva temples, the deity here is Hariharaâ€”a fusion of Hari (Vishnu) and Hara (Shiva).

The Tribal Heart

Odisha State Tribal Museum

To understand Odisha's gods, one must understand its people. The "High Hinduism" of the temples is built on the bedrock of tribal spirituality. Lord Jagannath himself began as Nila Madhava, a tribal deity.
