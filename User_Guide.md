## Site Navigation

### Main Navigation Menu

The site features a responsive navigation menu accessible from the header:

- **Home**: Returns to the hero section and introduction
- **Journal**: Scrolls to the main content sections (Days 1-4)
- **Itinerary**: Shows the timeline overview of the pilgrimage
- **Gallery**: Displays visual glimpses of the sacred sites
- **About**: Final reflections and conclusion
- **Blog**: Accesses the integrated blog content (added dynamically)

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices, the navigation menu is hidden by default. Tap the hamburger menu (☰) icon to reveal the navigation options.

### Skip Links

For accessibility, a "Skip to main content" link is available at the top of the page, allowing screen reader users and keyboard navigators to bypass the navigation.

## Blog Content Overview

The blog section integrates rich content about the Odisha Sacred Odyssey, including:

### Content Types

1. **Pilgrimage Journals**: Personal accounts of the 4-day spiritual journey
2. **Research Articles**: In-depth analysis of temples, history, and culture
3. **Thematic Content**: Organized by spiritual, historical, architectural, and cultural themes

### Content Organization

Content is structured around four thematic arcs:

1. **Spiritual Journey and Transformation**: Personal spiritual experiences and insights
2. **Historical and Mythological Exploration**: Historical context and mythological stories
3. **Architectural and Engineering Marvels**: Temple architecture and engineering analysis
4. **Cultural and Social Insights**: Cultural traditions and social practices

## Content View Modes

The blog offers three distinct viewing modes to suit different exploration preferences:

### Grid View (Default)

- Displays blog posts as cards in a responsive grid layout
- Each card shows:
  - Featured image
  - Post title
  - Publication date and reading time
  - Brief excerpt
  - Associated tags
- Click any card to open the full post in a modal

**How to access**: Click the "All Posts" button in the blog controls

### Timeline View

- Organizes content chronologically by pilgrimage day
- Shows the journey progression from Day 1 to Day 4
- Each day section contains relevant posts and research
- Maintains the narrative flow of the spiritual journey

**How to access**: Click the "Timeline View" button in the blog controls

### Thematic View

- Groups content by the four thematic arcs
- Each theme section includes:
  - Theme description
  - Related posts and research articles
  - Visual connections between related content
- Facilitates exploration by spiritual, historical, architectural, or cultural interests

**How to access**: Click the "Thematic View" button in the blog controls

## Search and Discovery

### Search Functionality

- **Location**: Search box in the blog controls section
- **Features**:
  - Real-time search as you type
  - Searches across titles, excerpts, content, tags, and categories
  - Case-insensitive matching
  - Instant filtering of displayed posts

**How to use**:
1. Click in the search box
2. Type your search terms (e.g., "Jagannath", "temple", "spiritual")
3. Results update automatically
4. Clear the search to return to full content

### Sidebar Filters

The blog sidebar provides additional discovery tools:

#### Thematic Arcs
- Lists the four main thematic categories
- Click any arc to explore related content
- Helps focus on specific aspects of the journey

#### Categories
- Groups content by type (e.g., "Spiritual Journey", "Historical Research")
- Click to filter posts by category

#### Tags
- Cloud of keywords associated with posts
- Popular tags are visually emphasized
- Click any tag to find related content

## Interactive Elements

### Blog Post Cards

- **Hover Effects**: Subtle animations on mouse hover
- **Click Interaction**: Opens full post in modal overlay

### Modal Reading Experience

When you click a blog post card, a modal opens with:

- **Full Post Content**: Complete article with formatting
- **Navigation Controls**:
  - Previous/Next buttons to browse posts sequentially
  - Keyboard navigation (arrow keys)
- **Post Metadata**: Date, author, reading time, location

**Modal Controls**:
- **Close**: Click the × button or press Escape
- **Navigate**: Use Previous/Next buttons or Left/Right arrow keys
- **Background Click**: Click outside content area to close

- **Loading Indicators**: Animated spinner during content loading
- **Progressive Enhancement**: Content loads smoothly without page refreshes
- **Error Handling**: Graceful fallbacks if content fails to load

## Step-by-Step User Instructions

### Getting Started

1. **Open the Website**: Navigate to the Odisha Sacred Odyssey TravelBlog
2. **Explore the Introduction**: Read the welcome section and overview
3. **Navigate to Blog**: Use the main menu or scroll to the blog section

### Browsing Content

1. **Choose View Mode**:
   - Click "All Posts" for grid overview
   - Click "Timeline View" for chronological journey
   - Click "Thematic View" for thematic exploration

2. **Search Content**:
   - Type in the search box
   - Results filter automatically
   - Clear search to see all posts

3. **Use Sidebar Filters**:
   - Click thematic arcs for theme-based browsing
   - Select categories for content type filtering
   - Click tags for keyword-based discovery

### Reading Posts

1. **Open Post**: Click any blog card
2. **Read Content**: Scroll through the full article in the modal
3. **Navigate Between Posts**:
   - Use Previous/Next buttons
   - Or press Left/Right arrow keys
4. **Close Modal**: Click × or press Escape

### Mobile Usage

1. **Access Menu**: Tap hamburger icon for navigation
2. **Touch Interactions**: Tap cards and buttons to interact
3. **Swipe Navigation**: Use touch gestures in modals (future enhancement)

## Troubleshooting

### Content Not Loading
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Search Not Working
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try different search terms
- Clear search and try again

### Modal Issues
- Close and reopen the modal
- Use keyboard shortcuts if mouse isn't working
- Check for browser extensions interfering
