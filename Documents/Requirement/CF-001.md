# CF-001: Social Following System

Status: [NEW]

## Overview
As a travel enthusiast, I need to follow other travelers and bloggers to build my personal network and stay updated on their latest adventures, tips, and stories through a customized feed.

## Business Value
Social following fosters community engagement and increases platform stickiness by creating personal connections. It encourages content creation, boosts user retention, and creates network effects that attract new users.

## Target Audience
- Travel bloggers building their audience
- Readers following favorite content creators
- Aspiring travelers learning from experienced ones

## Key Features
- **Follow/Unfollow Actions**: Simple interface to follow users and topics
- **Personalized Feed**: Chronological timeline of followed users' content
- **Follower Analytics**: View follower counts and growth trends
- **Activity Notifications**: Alerts for new content from followed accounts

## Success Metrics
- **Network Growth**: Average users follow 20+ accounts within 3 months
- **Engagement Rate**: 45% higher interaction with followed content
- **Retention Impact**: 35% reduction in churn for active followers

## Technical Considerations
- **Graph Database**: Efficient social relationship management
- **Feed Generation**: Optimized personalized content streams
- **Notification Scaling**: High-volume push and email delivery
- **Privacy Controls**: Granular visibility settings for follows

## Implementation Notes
- **Relationship Storage**: Scalable user relationship database
- **Feed Caching**: Intelligent caching for performance
- **Moderation Tools**: Prevent follow spam and harassment
- **Analytics Integration**: Track social engagement metrics

## Risks and Mitigations
- **Harassment Potential**: Mitigation - Blocking, reporting, and privacy features
- **Performance Issues**: Mitigation - Database optimization and sharding
