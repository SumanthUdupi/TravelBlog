# CD-003: Trending and Popular Content

Status: [NEW]

## Overview
As a reader wanting to stay current with travel trends, I need access to trending blogs, popular destinations, and viral travel stories that highlight what's currently capturing the community's attention and interest.

## Business Value
Trending content leverages social proof to increase engagement and discovery. It creates a sense of community activity, encourages content creation around popular topics, and helps users find timely, relevant travel information.

## Target Audience
- Trend-conscious travelers following popular destinations
- Social media users seeking viral travel stories
- Content creators wanting to understand current interests

## Key Features
- **Real-time Trending**: Dynamic trending content based on engagement velocity
- **Popular Categories**: Top destinations, travel styles, and authors by metrics
- **Time-based Views**: Daily, weekly, and all-time popular content
- **Social Indicators**: Incorporate shares, comments, and community votes

## Success Metrics
- **Engagement Rate**: 40% higher interaction on trending vs. regular content
- **Content Discovery**: 30% increase in exploration of trending topics
- **Session Duration**: 25% longer visits for trending content consumers

## Technical Considerations
- **Algorithm Design**: Sophisticated trending algorithms with time decay
- **Real-time Updates**: Live recalculation of trending scores
- **Scalability**: Efficient processing for high-volume engagement data
- **Transparency**: Clear criteria for trending determination

## Implementation Notes
- **Scoring Framework**: Multi-factor algorithm considering recency, engagement, and quality
- **Performance Optimization**: Cached trending data with periodic refreshes
- **Anti-manipulation**: Measures to prevent artificial trending inflation
- **Moderation Tools**: Human oversight for trending content curation

## Risks and Mitigations
- **Trend Manipulation**: Mitigation - Robust anti-gaming algorithms and community reporting
- **Content Quality**: Mitigation - Quality filters in trending calculations
- **Algorithm Bias**: Mitigation - Regular audits and adjustment of scoring weights
