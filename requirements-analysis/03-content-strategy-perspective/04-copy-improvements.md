# Copy Improvements & Rewrite Recommendations

## Summary
Actionable rewrite guidance to improve clarity, SEO performance, and cultural accuracy across existing copy.

## Key Problems Observed
- Long, dense paragraphs that reduce skimmability.
- Inconsistent use of local terms and translations.
- SEO opportunities missed in headings and meta descriptions.

## Rewrite Guidelines
- Use clear H2/H3 hierarchy matching user intent.
- Place the primary keyword in the H1/H2 and first paragraph without keyword stuffing.
- Break long paragraphs into short, scannable blocks.

## Example Revision
**Before:** "The Konark Sun Temple is an important monument. Visitors come to see sculptures and architecture."
**After:** "The Konark Sun Temple — a 13th-century UNESCO icon — is famed for its intricate stone carvings and chariot-shaped design. Visiting tips: arrive early, respect photography rules, and plan 90–120 minutes."

## Audit Process
- Run a readability and SEO scan for top pages
- Produce a prioritized rewrite backlog (high-impact pages first)
- Pair content writers with subject matter experts for cultural accuracy

## Templates & Tools
- Headline formula templates
- Snippet templates for meta descriptions (max 155 chars)
- Use Hemingway or Readable for readability checks

## Action Items
1. Create prioritized list of pages for rewriting based on traffic and importance
2. Draft copy using the templates and run accessibility/language checks
3. Publish with A/B variations where applicable and track performance
