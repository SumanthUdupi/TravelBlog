# CF-002: Commenting and Discussion System

Status: [NEW]

## Overview
As a reader engaging with travel content, I need a robust commenting system to share thoughts, ask questions, and discuss travel experiences with other community members on blog posts and articles.

## Business Value
Interactive discussions increase user engagement and time spent on platform by fostering community dialogue. They provide valuable feedback to content creators and help build social connections among users.

## Target Audience
- Active readers wanting to share opinions
- Travel bloggers seeking community feedback
- Users looking for travel advice and recommendations

## Key Features
- **Threaded Comments**: Nested discussion threads for organized conversations
- **Rich Media Support**: Embed images, links, and travel maps in comments
- **Moderation Tools**: Community guidelines and moderator controls
- **Engagement Metrics**: Like, reply, and share functionality for comments

## Success Metrics
- **Comment Rate**: 25% of readers leave comments on popular posts
- **Discussion Depth**: Average 3+ replies per discussion thread
- **User Retention**: 20% higher retention for active commenters

## Technical Considerations
- **Real-time Updates**: Live comment loading and notifications
- **Spam Prevention**: Automated filtering and manual moderation
- **Scalability**: Handle high-volume comment sections
- **Data Storage**: Efficient comment database with search capabilities

## Implementation Notes
- **Comment API**: RESTful API for comment management
- **Notification System**: Alert users of replies and mentions
- **Search Integration**: Comments searchable within content
- **Backup and Recovery**: Reliable comment data preservation

## Risks and Mitigations
- **Toxic Discussions**: Mitigation - AI moderation and community reporting
- **Spam Overload**: Mitigation - CAPTCHA, rate limiting, and user verification
