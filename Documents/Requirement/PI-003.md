# PI-003: Smart Content Curation

Status: [NEW]

## Overview
As a platform administrator, I need AI-powered content curation that automatically identifies, categorizes, and promotes high-quality travel content to maintain editorial standards and surface the best stories for readers.

## Business Value
Smart curation ensures consistent content quality, reduces manual moderation burden, and helps surface valuable content that might otherwise be overlooked. It enhances user experience through better content discovery and maintains platform credibility.

## Target Audience
- Platform editors managing content quality
- Readers seeking high-quality, relevant travel stories
- Content creators wanting fair visibility for their work

## Key Features
- **Quality Scoring**: AI algorithms assessing content quality and relevance
- **Automated Tagging**: Intelligent categorization of destinations, themes, and styles
- **Content Promotion**: Smart featuring of high-quality posts
- **Trend Detection**: Identification of emerging travel topics and destinations

## Success Metrics
- **Content Quality**: 50% reduction in low-quality content visibility
- **Discovery Rate**: 35% increase in engagement with curated content
- **Editorial Efficiency**: 60% reduction in manual curation time

## Technical Considerations
- **AI Training**: Machine learning models for quality assessment
- **Scalability**: Process large volumes of new content efficiently
- **Bias Mitigation**: Fair algorithms avoiding discrimination
- **Human Oversight**: Appeal processes and manual review options

## Implementation Notes
- **Hybrid Approach**: AI recommendations with human final decisions
- **Feedback Loop**: User engagement data improving AI accuracy
- **Transparency**: Clear criteria for content curation and promotion

## Risks and Mitigations
- **Algorithm Bias**: Mitigation - Diverse training data and regular audits
- **Over-reliance on AI**: Mitigation - Human oversight and appeal mechanisms
- **Content Diversity**: Mitigation - Algorithms promoting variety and inclusion
